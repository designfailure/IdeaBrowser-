# a16z Big Ideas 2026 — synthesis (Parts 1–2)

Below: **condensed thesis statements** by contributor theme (paraphrase for operator use).

## Infrastructure and data

- **Multimodal enterprise data:** Unstructured data is the bottleneck for RAG/agents; startups win by continuous clean/structure/validate/govern pipelines across PDFs, video, logs, email.  
- **Cyber hiring crisis → AI automation:** Automate level-1 triage so scarce humans hunt real threats.  
- **Agent-native infrastructure:** Human-speed backends break under recursive agent fan-out; need coordination, locking, policy, cold-start, concurrency by design.  
- **Creative multimodal tools:** Reference-driven video/scene continuation; model + app layer innovation.  
- **AI-native data stack:** Vectors + structured stores; semantic layer for agents; BI becomes agentic.  
- **Video as inhabitable environment:** Consistent world state for robotics, games, agents learning by doing.

## Growth and enterprise apps

- **System of record loses primacy:** Intelligence/execution layer becomes strategic; SoR tends toward commodity persistence.  
- **Vertical AI “multiplayer”:** Cross-party agents with permissions + shared context create moats via collaboration graph.  
- **Design for agents:** Machine legibility beats visual hierarchy for distribution and ops surfaces.  
- **End of screen-time KPI:** Outcome pricing and ROI proof replace hours-in-app.  
- **Consumer “year of me” / personalization at scale** (Speedrun): education, health, media individualized.  
- **World models + generative economies:** User co-authored worlds; training environments for agents/robots.  
- **AI-native university:** Adaptive institution design (referenced as forward-looking institution shape).

## American Dynamism (Part 2)

- **AI-native industrial base:** Simulation-first design, autonomous fleets, continuous monitoring of critical infrastructure.  
- **American factory renaissance:** Modular AI + skilled labor; permitting, design-for-manufacture, autonomy for dangerous tasks.  
- **Physical observability:** Billion+ cameras/sensors; privacy-preserving trusted fabric for civic/industrial visibility.  
- **Electro-industrial stack:** Power electronics, batteries, motors—atoms+bits coordination as national leverage.  
- **Autonomous labs:** Hypothesis to execution loop across disciplines.  
- **Industrial data crusade:** Capture/annotate/consent stack; incumbents’ process data as training advantage; startups sell coordination.

## Apps / fintech / consumer distribution

- **AI reinforces business models:** Outcome-linked models (e.g., contingency law) compound with proprietary data.  
- **ChatGPT as AI app store:** Apps SDK + mini-apps + large distribution (thesis: new consumer channel).  
- **Voice agents expand to full workflows** beyond wedge calls.  
- **Prompt-free proactive apps:** Invisible scaffolding; intent-triggered actions.  
- **Banking/insurance core rebuild:** AI-native cores normalize data; parallelize mortgage LOS-class workflows; category merge.  
- **Forward-deployed motions outside Silicon Valley:** Legacy verticals and SI markets.  
- **Enterprise orchestration layer:** Multi-agent “digital teams”; new roles (workflow designer, agent supervisor, governance).  
- **Consumer “see me” vs “help me”:** Retention via relational depth vs discrete jobs.  
- **New model primitives unlock greenfield:** Reasoning + multimodal + computer use enable products that could not exist before.
