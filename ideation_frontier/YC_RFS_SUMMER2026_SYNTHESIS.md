# YC Requests for Startups — Summer 2026 synthesis

**Source:** https://www.ycombinator.com/rfs (section headings as published; paraphrase + thematic grouping below).

## Meta-theme

YC frames **AI as foundation** (not a feature): software, services, silicon, and **physical-world** deployment.

## Theme buckets (from published RFS list)

1. **AI for low-pesticide agriculture** — precision vision, robotics, biological substitutes; yield + margin + regulatory pressure on chemistry.  
2. **AI-native discovery engines** — closed-loop scientific systems (design–make–test–analyze) beyond copilots; drug discovery, materials, protein engineering cited.  
3. **AI-native service companies** — sell outcomes, not seats: insurance brokerage, accounting/tax/audit, compliance, healthcare administration.  
4. **AI personalized medicine** — agents over EHR/genome/wearables; diagnostics cost curve; n-of-one therapies and FDA openness narrative.  
5. **Company brain** — consolidate fragmented operational knowledge into an executable “skills file” for agents (not search-only).  
6. **Counter-swarm defense** — layered sensors, fusion software, non-kinetic defeat, autonomy stack attacks; “distributed system” not single weapon.  
7. **Dynamic software interfaces** — users (via coding agents) become forward-deployed engineers; shipping primitives vs fixed UI.  
8. **Electronics / inference in space** — radiation/thermal/mass-aware inference demand.  
9. **Hardware supply chain velocity** — US iteration gap vs Shenzhen; fast parts, design–mfg integration (names Hlabs W26, Prototyping.io P26 as examples in text).  
10. **Industrial capabilities in space** — regolith electrolysis, molten 3D print structures.  
11. **Inference chips for agent workflows** — KV-cache-friendly, tool-loop silicon + compiler story (Groq narrative).  
12. **SaaS challengers** — AI-native rebuild of large legacy categories (ERP, EDA, industrial control).  
13. **Software for agents** — MCP/API/CLI-first products; “Make Something Agents Want.”  
14. **Selling to huge enterprises** — F100 pilots earlier; depth with small teams.  
15. **Supply chain 2.0 for semiconductors** — multi-tier visibility, export control dynamism, HBM/packaging bottlenecks.  
16. **AI operating system for companies** — record everything, closed-loop learning vs stitched glue code.

## Implication for ideation

RFS is **explicit demand signaling** from YC—not exclusive funding criteria. High-alpha ideation often sits **orthogonal** to RFS (underserved wedge) or **one layer below** RFS (infrastructure enabler the RFS assumes exists).
