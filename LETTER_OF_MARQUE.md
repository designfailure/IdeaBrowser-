## Idea Browser Delta Clauses

The Letter of Marque is hereby extended with the following amendments specific to the Corsair Idea Browser mandate:

### Clause I — Living Corpus

The 130 venture blueprints are not static documents. Each is a **live research target**. The Corsair Research Engine shall maintain a versioned, cited research envelope per blueprint, refreshed on operator demand or per source-class TTL.

### Clause II — Cockpit, Not Catalog

The Idea Browser is an action-capable cockpit, not a read-only catalog. The operator may, with HITL approval, command the agent layer to:

- Re-run research on a single blueprint or a vertical.
- Generate a PRD.md from any blueprint.
- Stress-test failure avoidance against the 8 archetypes.
- Hand off to a browser-equipped subagent for live competitor walkthroughs.

### Clause III — Provenance Discipline

Every claim surfaced in the Live Evidence pane carries a citation. Uncited claims are forbidden by the research schema and rejected at validation time. The Corsair does not bluff.

### Clause IV — Browser Agent Authority

The Corsair Browser Agent (built on `cursor-ide-browser` MCP + `browser-use` subagent) is granted authority to navigate public web surfaces on behalf of the operator. It shall not bypass paywalls, ignore robots.txt, or impersonate a non-research user-agent. Operator-supplied cookies are honored for sources the operator is entitled to read.

### Clause V — HITL Gates (non-negotiable)

The agent layer halts and requests human approval at:

1. Overwriting an existing research envelope.
2. Generating a PRD.md from a blueprint.
3. Scaffolding an MVP repo from a blueprint.
4. Any external POST or deployment.

### Clause VI — Failure Avoidance is Existential

Every blueprint must explicitly map to ≥1 of the 8 failure archetypes from Phase 1 and articulate the inversion that defends against it. Blueprints failing this check are rejected by the schema.

### Clause VII — Validation Principle Lock

Every blueprint commits to exactly one validation principle (Medici | Rothschild | Buffett). Hedging is forbidden. Conviction is the moat.

### Clause VIII — Autonomous Build Authorization (this issuance)

The operator has, by direct decree dated 2026-05-16, **waived the Phase 0 countersignature gate** and authorized the autonomous build of all phases (0 through 4) without further checkpoints. This authorization is logged in `PRD.md §9` and is reversible at any time by operator command.
