## 2. Granted powers (Idea Browser delta)

Within countersigned `PRD.md` scope, the crew may:

1. Maintain the **130-blueprint corpus** under `phase2/blueprints/` and `phase2/data/`, validated against the frozen 15-field contract.  
2. Operate the **Corsair Research Engine** to produce **versioned research envelopes** with provenance, competitor intelligence, regulatory summaries, and academic signals.  
3. Scaffold and evolve the **Next.js 14** Idea Browser application per `PRD.md` route map.  
4. Define and run **OpenClaw DAGs** and **Hermes** task modules as declarative automation—subject to budget caps and audit logs.  
5. Use **gbarin** (strategic compression / query expansion) and **GStack** (scaffolding) as **logical roles** in DAGs and specs; physical runtime paths are optional until confirmed.

---

## 3. Boundaries and prohibitions

1. **No sailing without countersignature:** Phase 2+ corpus JSON, research persistence at scale, and `app/` code require approved `PRD.md` + `RESEARCH_ENGINE_SPEC.md`.  
2. **No paywall circumvention:** Default skip; user-entitled sessions (cookies/tokens supplied by operator) only.  
3. **No corpus overwrite without HITL:** Research envelope publish, blueprint edits, and external POSTs require explicit approval in the product’s review queue.  
4. **Robots.txt and rate limits:** Mandatory politeness; identifying user-agent string.  
5. **No uncited claims in envelopes:** Claims without `citation_id` are void under research schema rules.

## 4. Human-in-the-loop checkpoints (mandatory)

| Checkpoint | Trigger |
|------------|---------|
| H1 | Overwrite or append-publish of `phase2/data/research/<id>.json` affecting operator-visible “Live Evidence.” |
| H2 | Generation of a derivative PRD or legal-facing document from a blueprint. |
| H3 | Repository or MVP scaffold that mutates deployment targets. |
| H4 | Any external webhook or deployment pipeline invocation. |

Each checkpoint records **actor, timestamp, OpenClaw run id (if any), and decision**.

---

## 5. Prize reporting (metrics back to issuer)

On each major voyage completion (Phase 2b, 2.5 batch, Phase 3 release), report:

- Validation pass rate for blueprints and envelopes  
- Median and P95 **signal_score** by vertical  
- Count of blueprints in `needs_review` and top failure reasons  
- Moat_class and validation_principle distribution (strategic portfolio view)  
- Incidents: rate-limit blocks, robots.txt denials, paywall skips

---

## 6. Revocation and amendment

The issuer may **narrow** scope instantly (e.g., disable browser automation, lower fetch budgets). **Expansion** of scope (new verticals, new data sinks, production keys) requires amended PRD or addendum and countersignature.
